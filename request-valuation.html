Thinking of selling or letting your property?

This is a free service and there is no obligation to engage our services, so if you need a valuation for either selling or renting your property we’re ready to help. You can book a valuation with any of our offices. Once we have your details we will give you a call at a suitable time to you.

Full Estate Agency service: €1599+VAT

The days of paying an estate agent several thousand Euro to sell a property are gone. Our experienced, qualified estate agents will handle your property’s sale from start to finish. We will get you the best price for your property but our fee is just €1599+VAT, saving you thousands.

Selling Your Home - Market Appraisal

Before considering bringing a property to the market to either sell or with a view to earning a potential rental income, it is essential that you get a real feel for its market value – that’s where we come in. Our team of valuation experts have an in-depth knowledge of the property market in your area and will be happy to guide you with their expertise and local knowledge. Often referred to as a Market Appraisal our valuation will provide you with that all-important guide price on which to make an informed decision when it comes to selling your home.

If you are thinking of selling, fill out the above form and your local office will be in touch.

Banking, Legal And Revenue Valuations

There are times when you may need a valuation on a property for Banking, Legal or Revenue related reasons. Our dedicated Residential Valuations Team provides a professional and responsive service, using their detailed market knowledge and comprehensive property database for comparative purposes, providing valuations for clients primarily in the following key areas:

  • Probate
  • Family Law
  • Expert Witness
  • Red Book/ Blue Book Valuations for Banking and Mortgage purposes
  • Revenue-related valuations
  • Family Transfer
  • Capital Gains Tax
  • Capital Acquisition Tax
  • Self-Administered Pension Funds
  • HSE Fair Deal Scheme

To avail of one of the above services please contact us at +353 01 23456 765 or resivals@redcoralrealty.ie
